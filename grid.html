The Green Mile

The Green Mile is set in a 1930s Louisiana prison and follows Paul Edgecomb, a death row corrections officer, as he encounters John Coffey, a physically imposing but gentle Black man accused of murdering two young girls. The story explores themes of compassion, injustice, and the burden of carrying miracles in a deeply flawed world.

Avengers InfinityWar

Avengers: Infinity War brings together heroes from across the Marvel Universe to stop the powerful warlord Thanos. The film ends in devastating loss, setting the stage for a desperate fight for redemption in the next chapter.

The Shawshank Redempetion

The Shawshank Redemption tells the story of Andy Dufresne, a quiet banker wrongly convicted of murdering his wife and her lover. The story is a powerful tale of hope, resilience, and redemption against all odds.

The NUN

The Nun is a horror story set in 1952 Romania, where a young nun takes her own life at a remote abbey. The film is a chilling prequel to The Conjuring universe, revealing the origins of one of its most terrifying entities.
