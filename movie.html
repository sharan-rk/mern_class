INTERSTELLAR

Interstellar follows Cooper, a former NASA pilot turned farmer in a near-future Earth ravaged by climate disaster. When a secret NASA mission seeks to find a new habitable planet, Cooper joins a team of astronauts who travel through a wormhole near Saturn. As they navigate time dilation, alien worlds, and personal sacrifices, Cooper races against time to save humanity — and reconnect with his daughter Murph, who holds the key to solving gravity and ensuring survival on Earth. The story blends science, emotion, and a profound exploration of love across time and space.

The Green Mile

The Green Mile is set in a 1930s Louisiana prison and follows Paul Edgecomb, a death row corrections officer, as he encounters John Coffey, a physically imposing but gentle Black man accused of murdering two young girls. As Coffey awaits execution, strange and miraculous events begin to occur, revealing his supernatural healing powers. Paul and his fellow guards come to believe in Coffey's innocence and divine gift, but justice moves forward regardless. The story explores themes of compassion, injustice, and the burden of carrying miracles in a deeply flawed world.

Avengers InfinityWar

Avengers: Infinity War brings together heroes from across the Marvel Universe to stop the powerful warlord Thanos. As he hunts down the six Infinity Stones to gain god-like control over the universe, the Avengers, Guardians of the Galaxy, and their allies fight to prevent him from wiping out half of all life. Despite their efforts, Thanos succeeds in collecting all the stones and, with a snap of his fingers, causes half the population to vanish. The film ends in devastating loss, setting the stage for a desperate fight for redemption in the next chapter.

The Shawshank Redempetion

The Shawshank Redemption tells the story of Andy Dufresne, a quiet banker wrongly convicted of murdering his wife and her lover. Sentenced to life at Shawshank Prison, he endures brutal conditions but forms a deep friendship with fellow inmate Ellis "Red" Redding. Over the years, Andy uses his intelligence to help the warden with money laundering while secretly planning his escape. After nearly two decades, he breaks free through a tunnel he secretly dug and exposes the prison’s corruption. The story is a powerful tale of hope, resilience, and redemption against all odds.

The NUN

The Nun is a horror story set in 1952 Romania, where a young nun takes her own life at a remote abbey. The Vatican sends Father Burke and a novitiate, Sister Irene, to investigate. They uncover a dark secret: the abbey is haunted by a malevolent demon named Valak, who takes the form of a terrifying nun. As they delve deeper into the mystery, they battle supernatural forces and try to seal the evil back where it came from. The film is a chilling prequel to The Conjuring universe, revealing the origins of one of its most terrifying entities.

American Physco

American Psycho follows Patrick Bateman, a wealthy and successful investment banker in 1980s Manhattan who leads a double life as a psychopathic killer. Beneath his polished exterior and obsession with materialism, Bateman spirals into violent and disturbing behavior, blurring the lines between reality and his dark fantasies. The story is a satirical and chilling critique of consumerism, identity, and the emptiness of modern yuppie culture.

The Human Centipede

The Human Centipede is a horror film about a deranged surgeon who kidnaps three tourists and surgically joins them mouth-to-anus to create a “human centipede.” The film explores extreme body horror and psychological terror as the victims endure unimaginable suffering. It’s a shocking and controversial story that pushes the boundaries of horror with its grotesque concept.
